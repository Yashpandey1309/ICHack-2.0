Recycling

THERMAL RECYCLING

One of the most recent techniques is thermal recycling. This process uses heat to convert waste into fuel, which can then be used to generate energy or in useful materials.

RECYCLING

MECHANICAL RECYCLING

Another technique that has gained popularity in recent years is mechanical recycling. This process uses machinery to separate the different components of waste, such as paper, glass, and metal.

Technology

Motion Sensors

A motion sensor is a technology that is capable of detecting any physical movement within a particular environment. The device turns off lights in a room when not in use.
